Top things to do

Chui Chak

also known as Paddy Fields, is a scenic destination in Perak, showcasing vast stretches of lush, green rice fields.

  • Experience the beauty of the countryside, where traditional farming techniques and serene landscapes come together.
  • Capture stunning photos of the fields, particularly during the harvest season, when the golden crops create a picturesque view.
  • Immerse yourself in the tranquil rural setting, perfect for those seeking a quiet escape from the hustle and bustle.

Pangkor Island

a tropical island off the coast of Perak, is renowned for its pristine beaches, clear waters, and laid-back charm.

  • Relax on beaches like Teluk Nipah and Pasir Bogak, perfect for swimming and snorkeling.
  • Explore hidden coves and trails for hiking amidst lush landscapes.
  • Savor fresh seafood and enjoy breathtaking sunsets.
  • Experience the ultimate tropical getaway.

Tasik Cermin Ipoh

also known as the "Mirror Lake", is a hidden gem in Ipoh, surrounded by towering limestone cliffs and lush greenery.

  • Experience the beauty of the countryside, where traditional farming techniques and serene landscapes come together.
  • Capture stunning photos of the fields, particularly during the harvest season, when the golden crops create a picturesque view.
  • Immerse yourself in the tranquil rural setting, perfect for those seeking a quiet escape from the hustle and bustle.

Gopeng Glamping Park

offers a unique escape into nature, surrounded by lush greenery and scenic hills.

  • Stay in cozy glamping tents equipped with modern comforts.
  • Engage in thrilling activities like white-water rafting, caving, and jungle trekking, or simply relax by the campfire under the starry sky.
  • Perfect for families, couples, and adventurers.
  • Itâ€™s an ideal spot to reconnect with nature and create unforgettable memories.

Concubine Lane

nestled in the heart of Ipoh's Old Town, is known for its rich history, charming heritage buildings, and bustling atmosphere.

  • Discover unique attractions, including vibrant murals and quaint shophouses selling handmade crafts, snacks, and souvenirs.
  • Stroll through the lane's historical alleys, once a haven for tin miners, and admire its preserved architecture and cultural charm.
  • Indulge in local delights like Ipoh White Coffee, Tau Fu Fah, and freshly-made Kaya Puffs from nearby traditional coffee shops and bakeries.
