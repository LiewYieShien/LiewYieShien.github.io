Top things to do

George Town

a UNESCO World Heritage Site, is known for its colonial architecture, vibrant street art, and cultural diversity.

  • Explore iconic street art, including murals like "Children on a Bicycle."
  • Visit historical sites such as Peranakan Mansion and
    (The Blue Mansion).
  • Indulge in street food like Char Kway Teow, Assam Laksa, and Cendol at Gurney Drive or Lebuh Chulia.

Penang Hills

This hill station offers breathtaking panoramic views of the island and a cooler climate, perfect for nature lovers.

  • Take the Penang Hill funicular train to the top.
  • Walk along The Habitat's Canopy Walk or explore nature trails.
  • Visit the historical and its gardens.
  • Enjoy an English tea experience with a view at David Brown’s restaurant.

Top things to do

Kek Lok Si Temple

One of Southeast Asia's largest Buddhist temples , Kek Lok Si is known for its stunning architecture and peaceful ambiance.

  • Admire the 30-meter-tall statue of the Goddess of Mercy (Kuan Yin).
  • Light candles or make wishes at the temple's prayer hall.
  • Experience the magical sight of the illuminated temple during Chinese New Year.

Penang National Park

A heaven for outdoor enthusiasts, this park offers unspoiled natural landscapes and diverse wildlife.

  • Hike through lush trails to the famous lighthouse at Cape Rachado.
  • Visit Monkey Beach for swimming, picnics, or jet skiing.
  • Take a boat ride to explore hidden coves and beaches.
  • Spot wildlife like monkeys, lizards, and exotic birds.

ESCAPE Penang

An exciting adventure park with activities suitable for all ages, blending outdoor fun and physical challenges.

  • Experience thrilling rides like the world’s longest water slide and ziplining.
  • Conquer obstacle courses or try tree-top adventures.
  • Relax with tubing or swimming in natural pools.
  • Let kids enjoy safe, age-appropriate activities in a dedicated section.

Penang is a culinary paradise with must-try dishes showcasing its rich multicultural heritage.

Monday to Sunday: 12:00 PM – 2:30 PM (Lunch)
Monday to Sunday: 6:30 PM – 10:00 PM (Dinner)

RM15-40 per person

Operating Hours:

7:30 AM – 1:00 PM (Closed on Mondays).

RM5–10 per person
