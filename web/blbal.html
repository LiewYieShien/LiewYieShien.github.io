Top things to do

George Town

A UNESCO World Heritage Site, George Town is known for its colonial architecture, vibrant street art, and cultural diversity.

  • Explore iconic street art, including murals like "Children on a Bicycle."
  • Visit historical sites such as Pinang Peranakan Mansion and The Blue Mansion.
  • Indulge in local street food like Char Kway Teow, Assam Laksa, and Cendol at Gurney Drive or Lebuh Chulia.
