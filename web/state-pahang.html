PAHANG

Top things to do

Cameron Highlands

a serene hill station in Pahang, is famous for its cool climate , picturesque plantations, and vibrant flora.

  • Visit iconic tea estates like BOH Tea Plantation, where you can enjoy breathtaking views and savor freshly brewed tea.
  • Explore colorful flower gardens, lush strawberry farms, and bustling local markets selling fresh produce and handmade goods.
  • Take scenic nature trails through mossy forests and enjoy the peaceful charm of this highland retreat, perfect for relaxation and rejuvenation.

Tioman Island

located off the coast of Pahang, is a tropical paradise known for itspristine beaches, crystal-clear waters , and vibrant marine life.

  • Explore vibrant coral reefs and marine life through world-class snorkeling and scuba diving.
  • Unwind on serene beaches like Salang, Juara, and Tekek.
  • Embark on adventurous hikes through lush rainforests.
  • Discover cascading waterfalls amidst nature.

Colmar Tropicale

located in Berjaya Hills, is a charming French-themed village inspired by the picturesque town of Colmar in Alsace, France.

  • Stroll through the cobblestone streets surrounded by colorful half-timbered buildings, creating a magical European ambiance.
  • Enjoy activities such as horse riding, exploring the serene Japanese Village, and visiting the tranquil Botanical Garden.
  • Savor delectable French-inspired cuisine and pastries while soaking in the unique architecture and cool highland atmosphere.

Penang Hills

This hill station offers breathtaking panoramic views of the island and a cooler climate, perfect for nature lovers.

  • Take the Penang Hill funicular train to the top.
  • Walk along The Habitat's Canopy Walk or explore nature trails.
  • Visit the historical and its gardens.
  • Enjoy an English tea experience with a view at David Brown’s restaurant.
